Since 1946

Four generations at the Carr Cheese Factory

The Gile family has operated the Carr Cheese Factory since 1946. The recipes, the vats, and the stubborn standards have been handed down ever since — which is how a small shop in Cuba City ends up with more than 200 awards and a Wisconsin State Champion title.

Everything in the outlet case is cut, wrapped, and priced the way a factory store should be: fresh and fair.
